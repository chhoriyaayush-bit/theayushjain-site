Zindagi Ek Ghazal

This book contains poems that express love, pain, life, heartbreak, friendship, and many emotions — a dictionary of words, but also a dictionary of a poet's mind: a glimpse of how he sees life, how he feels the pain.

Zindagi Ek Ghazal is a musical odyssey through the human heart, divided into five acts — the heights of Love (Mohabbat), the quiet depths of Solitude (Akelapan), the foundation of Friendship (Dosti), the tension of Waiting (Intezaar), and finally, a celestial sanctuary in Love in the Stars. A song of a soul that finds rhythm in every tear and joy in every shared smile.

About the Author

Ayush Chhoriya is a contemporary poet capturing the raw, timeless symphony of the human heart. In his debut collection, Zindagi Ek Ghazal, he blends the lyrical beauty of classic expression with modern verse — exploring love, solitude, friendship, and waiting, before arriving at a boundless sanctuary among the stars. His work is a shared journey toward finding destiny and connection, in human bonds and beyond.

"The true measure of love is not the joy of finding, but the lonely wisdom gathered while navigating the dark, until you realize the city of stars was built from the patience of your own waiting heart."

The Resolve Of 52 Years!!

On India's Women's Cricket World Cup win — a 52-year wait, finally answered.

Read full post

In English

The story was rooted to the earth, the dream to touch the sky. ✨
In every breath was the fire of passion, every sorrow accounted for, nearby..... 💯
This five-decade long journey, a play of light and shade,
Where every wall of society had to be broken, a fusion of spirit made...💪

The past's defeat they turned not to grief, but to strength and might. 🔥
No tears in their eyes, but a dream to shine so bright.....💖
The echo was of every lane, every field, from domestic grounds did rise,
This was the resolve of Woman Power, witnessed now by all the world's eyes..... 🌍

Each morning's rays brought more than just the day's start, 🌅
It was years of penance they quietly played a part.....🧘‍♀️
But the heart's beat finally lent its voice to the silent call,
Bondage broke, new Passion touched the Sky of all!! 🚀

This victory is not just a sight for the eyes, it's a union of the soul. 🫂
It is the final bridge for every deferred goal.....
Now in Every Home rises with pride, the daughters of Bharat's name,
They changed the game, changed the time, fulfilling a glorious aim.....👏

That Cup which for 52 years stood afar and alone, ⏳
These Women brought Victory home, their passion fiercely shown. 🥳
Their hard work, their dedication became immortal,
History filled new pages with the colour of gold, so eternal. 🥇✨

Manzil ka Junoon 🔥

On restlessness, ambition, and refusing to slow down until the goal is reached.

Read full post

In English

The clamor of my thoughts—how can sleep now reside? 🌙
Not on my eyelids, but on the goal, my sight is tied....✨
The path that I've chosen is surely not easy or mild, ⛰️
But the fire to truly live has made every road reconciled....🔥

The dreams waiting there for me send out their potent cue,
They bring courage's message every hour a new.....💪
There are great and mighty things that I have to do, 🏆
Before which every closed door bends and breaks through....🚪

To pause, to tire? That's simply not in my lexicon, 🙅‍♂️
On my heart's book, it's just a story of moving on.....📖
Every victory will become my identity in this dawn, 🥇
And every moment will whisper, "Yes, this life is truly your own."...👑

I am separate from the crowd—that is my philosophy, 🚶
I am drawing the luminous line for what my life will be....💡
The walls of fear must be smashed for the victory, 🔨
I give every hardship a new resolve and tenacity.....🦁

Even in my silence, there is a warrior's shout, 📢
Every tomorrow must better what today is about....📈
Even the waves of the ocean will cease and die out, 🌊🚫
Because now I intend to make the sky itself bow down....🌌⬇️

In Hindi

Shor hai khayalon ka—ab neend ka silsila kaisa? 🌙
Palkon pe nahi, manzil pe nazar tiki hai ✨....
Jo rahguzar chuni hai, woh aasaan nahi, ⛰️
Magar us jeene ki aag se har raah chamki hai....🔥

Sapne jo mere liye raasta dekh rahe hain,
Woh har ghadi himmat ka paigaam dete hain....💪
Mujhe karni hai woh har ek aisi badi baat, 🏆
Jiske aage har band darwaze jhukte hain....🚪

Thamna, thakna? Ye to ab lafzon mein nahi, 🙅‍♂️
Dil ki kitaab pe bas aage badhne ki kahani hai.....📖
Har jeet meri pehchaan banegi is jahaan mein, 🥇
Aur har lamha kahega, "Haan, ye teri hi zindagani hai."....👑

Bheed se alag hoon, ye mera falsafa hai, 🚶
Main roshni ki lakeer khud kheench raha hoon.....💡
Dar ki deewaron ko tod kar aage badhna hai, 🔨
Main har mushkil ko naya hausla de raha hoon....🦁

Meri khaamoshi mein bhi lalkaar ki awaaz hai, 📢
Har kal mere aaj se behtar banana hai.....📈
Ye samandar ki lehrein bhi ruk jaayengi, 🌊🚫
Kyunki ab to aasmaan ko hi jhukana hai.....🌌⬇️

Her!! ✨👀

A tribute — her eyes, her voice, her quiet strength, in two languages.

Read full post

In Hindi

Uski Aankh, woh gehra samandar hai mere liye, 🌊
Jahaan Ishq ka pehla sachh aakar thehre..... 🤍
Uske Hoth, woh subah ki kirnain hain, hansti hui, ☀️
Jismein pyaar ka pakeezah ehsaas lehre..... ✨

Uski Surat, chaandni jaisi saaf aur haseen lage, 🌕
Yaad-e-wafa ka diya bankar jalta hai.....🕯️
Uske Baal, ghani ghatayein hain, sukoon ki chhaon de, 🌳
Jahaan har thaka hua musafir aakar palta hai..... 😌

Uski Awaaz, jharne ki dhun hai, jo rooh ko shifa de, 💧
Har guzre hue lamhe ko dobara woh jeeti hai..... 🎶
Uske Jhumke, hawa se hilte patton ki goonj hain, 🍃
Jo aane waale kal ki khabar chura kar peeti hai..... 🔮

Uski Nazakat, phool ki tehni jaisi narm hai magar khaas, 🌸
Woh sabr hai jo toofan mein bhi harr roz nazar aaye..... 🧘
Uska Andaaz, bahaar ki khushbu jaisa aam nahi, 🌷
Woh manzil hai, jise har ek dil paane ko taras jaaye..... 🎯

In English

Her eyes, they are a deep ocean for me, 🌊
Where the first truth of love comes to reside..... 🤍
Her lips, they are the morning's bright sunbeam, smilingly, ☀️
In which the pure sense of passion does glide..... ✨

Her face, like moonlight, seems spotless and fine, 🌕
A lamp of true loyalty that steadily burns..... 🕯️
Her hair, they are thick clouds, offering shade divine, 🌳
Where every tired soul finds shelter and turns..... 😌

Her voice, a waterfall's melody, healing the heart, 💧
Every moment that passed, she brings back to life..... 🎶
Her earrings, the sound of leaves fluttering apart, 🍃
Stealing news of tomorrow, away from the strife..... 🔮

Her softness, like a flower stem, gentle yet rare, 🌸
That patience is seen even when fierce tempests rise..... 🧘
Her manner, not common like spring fragrance in the air, 🌷
She is the destination every heart longs to surprise..... 🎯
